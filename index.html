Professor Owl says

Let’s do taxes one tiny step at a time. I’ll show you what each line means, where to find it, and a quick example.

Step 1: Get your papers

What it means: Gather your W-2s, 1099s, and receipts.

Tip 1: Put everything in one folder.
Tip 2: Make sure you have your Social Security number.
Tip 3: Check your bank info for direct deposit.

Step 2: Find your income

What it means: Write down the money you earned.

Tip 1: Look at Box 1 on your W-2.
Tip 2: Add any side-job money from 1099 forms.
Tip 3: Use a calculator so you do not guess.

Step 3: Use deductions and credits

What it means: These can lower your tax bill.

Tip 1: Deductions lower the money that gets taxed.
Tip 2: Credits lower the tax itself.
Tip 3: Credits are usually the bigger win.

Professor Owl says: Double-check every number, sign your return, and click the finish button when you are ready.
