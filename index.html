🦉 Professor Owl’s 1040 Tax Guide

Simple steps anyone in 7th grade can understand

📄 Step-by-Step 1040 Instructions

1. Gather your W-2 and 1099 forms
2. Write your name and SSN on Form 1040
3. Enter your total income
4. Add interest and dividends
5. Subtract adjustments (student loans, IRA)
6. Find Adjusted Gross Income (AGI)
7. Choose standard deduction or itemized
8. Calculate taxable income
9. Use tax table to find tax amount
10. Apply tax credits
11. Subtract payments already made
12. Find refund or amount owed
13. Double-check numbers
14. Sign your return
15. Submit online or mail
🦉 Professor Owl Tip: Always check numbers twice before submitting!

🧠 Professor Owl Reminder Rules

16. Never rush your tax form
17. Keep all documents in one folder
18. Save receipts for deductions
19. Use correct Social Security number
20. Check spelling of your name
21. Always report ALL income
22. Don’t guess numbers
23. Use IRS instructions if confused
24. Ask for help if needed
25. File before deadline
