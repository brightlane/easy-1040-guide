3 Form 1040: Top Lines 1–6

Enter wages, salaries, tips, interest, dividends, and other income following IRS instructions.

W-2 Box 1: $42,000 → Form 1040 line 1
Include all wages from W-2 forms. Do not include pre-tax 401k contributions.
Taxable Interest: $500 → line 2b; Tax-Exempt: $100 → line 2a
Report interest from banks, bonds, CDs. Tax-exempt interest is reported but not taxed.

Daily Tax Tips:
