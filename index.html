🗂Step 1: Gather Your Papers

  • W-2 from your employer
  • 1099 forms (if you did side work)
  • Social Security Number
  • Any other income documents
[ W-2 ] + [ 1099 ] + [ SSN ] = Ready to Start

Explanation: Collect all your income documents first. Missing anything may delay filing.

✏️Step 2: Fill Personal Info

Name: Jane Doe SSN: XXX-XX-0000 Filing Status: Single

Explanation: Single = not married. Head of Household = you support children/dependents. Filing status affects your taxes.

💵Step 3: Enter Income

Wages: $40,000 Side Job: $500 Interest: $200 Total Income: $40,700

Explanation: Enter every source of income. Total income goes on Form 1040 line 1-7.

📊Step 4: Adjust Income

Total Income: $40,700 IRA Deduction: -$2,000 Student Loan Interest: -$500 AGI: $38,200

Explanation: Adjustments lower your taxable income. Your AGI is used to determine credits and deductions.

📉Step 5: Choose Deductions

AGI: $38,200 Standard Deduction: -$13,850 Taxable Income: $24,350

Explanation: Standard deduction is easiest. Itemized deduction may save more if you have big expenses.

💰Step 6: Apply Credits

Tax Before Credits: $2,300 Child Tax Credit: -$1,000 Education Credit: -$200 Tax After Credits: $1,100

Explanation: Credits directly reduce the tax you owe. Always check eligibility.

🎉Step 7: Filing & Refund

Taxes Paid: $1,500 Tax Owed: $1,100 Refund: $400 🎉

Explanation: If you paid more than owed, you get a refund. Otherwise, pay the difference.
