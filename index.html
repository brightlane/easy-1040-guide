5 Self-Employment & Rental Income

Report business and rental income using Schedule C, SE, and E.

Gross Income: $8,000 Business Expenses: $2,500 Net Profit: $5,500 → 1040 line 8
Tip: Keep receipts and track expenses carefully to maximize deductions.
Net Profit: $5,500 Self-Employment Tax: $5,500 × 15.3% = $841.50 Deductible Portion (50%): $420.75 → 1040 line 10
Rental Income: $6,000 Expenses: $1,800 Net Rental Income: $4,200 → 1040 line 8
Tip: Include mortgage interest, property taxes, and maintenance expenses.
