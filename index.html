🦉 Professor Hootington’s Tax Filing Guide

✅ PART 1 — GATHERING YOUR DOCUMENTS

📄 Step 1: Collect your income forms

  • W-2s from your employer(s)
  • 1099 forms for freelance or interest/dividend income
  • Other income records (rental, side hustles, tips)
Tip: Organize everything by type. Professor Hootington says: “A tidy desk is a tidy tax return!”

📄 Step 2: Collect your expense & deduction records

  • Receipts for charitable donations
  • Medical expenses (if itemizing)
  • Education expenses (tuition, books)
Tip: Keep digital copies to avoid losing receipts.

📄 Step 3: Gather personal info & ID numbers

  • Social Security numbers (yours, spouse’s, dependents)
  • Bank account info for direct deposit
Tip: Double-check all numbers—one wrong digit can delay your refund!
