1️⃣ Line 1 – Wages, Salaries, Tips

Enter total wages from all W-2 forms. Example: Employer A: $30,000 Employer B: $10,000 Total: $40,000
💡 Tip: Double-check your SSN and name match exactly your Social Security card.

2️⃣ Line 2 – Taxable Interest

Add taxable interest from banks or investment accounts. Bank A: $50 Bank B: $150 Total: $200
💡 Tip: Exclude tax-free municipal bonds.

3️⃣ Line 3 – Dividends

Include ordinary and qualified dividends. Example: Ordinary: $100 Qualified: $50 Total: $150
💡 Tip: Qualified dividends are taxed at lower rates.
