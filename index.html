How to Fill Out a 1040 Tax Form

Simple Step-by-Step Guide for Beginners

This guide will show you how to complete the IRS Form 1040 in the easiest way possible. No confusing words. Just simple steps.

👉 Start Your Taxes the Easy Way

Step 1: Get Your Documents

  • W-2 (job income)
  • 1099 (side income)
  • Social Security Number
[ W-2 ] + [ 1099 ] + [ SSN ] = Ready

Step 2: Fill In Your Info

Name: Jane Smith Filing Status: Single SSN: XXX-XX-0000
Make sure this matches your official records.

Step 3: Enter Income

W-2 Income: $40,000 Side Job: $5,000 ------------------ Total Income: $45,000
👉 Automatically Add Your Income

Step 4: Adjust Income

Total Income: $45,000 Adjustments: -$2,000 ------------------ AGI: $43,000

Step 5: Subtract Deduction

AGI: $43,000 Standard Deduction: -$13,850 ------------------ Taxable Income: $29,150

Step 6: Calculate Tax

Taxable Income → Tax = $3,200
👉 Let the Software Do This For You

Step 7: Apply Credits

Tax: $3,200 Credits: -$1,000 ------------------ New Tax: $2,200

Step 8: Payments

Paid: $3,000 Tax Owed: $2,200 ------------------ Refund: $800 🎉

Step 9: File Your Return

Review everything, sign, and submit.
