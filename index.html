🦉 Professor Owl’s Easy Tax Guide

✅ PART 1 — Get Your Papers Ready

Step 1: Income Papers

  • W-2 = money from your job
  • 1099 = extra money (side jobs, bank interest)
  • Other money (tips, small jobs)
Tip: Put everything in one folder.

Step 2: Deductions (Things that save money)

  • Charity donations
  • Medical bills
  • School costs
Tip: Keep receipts!

Step 3: Personal Info

  • Social Security numbers
  • Bank account
Tip: Check numbers carefully.

✅ PART 2 — Filling Out Your Taxes

Step 4: Add Your Income (Lines 4–8)

  • Line 4: Interest (bank money)
  • Line 5: Dividends (stocks)
  • Line 6: Retirement money
  • Line 7: Social Security
  • Line 8: Other income
Think of this like adding all your money together.

Step 5: Adjust Your Income (Line 9)

  • Student loan interest
  • IRA savings
  • Business costs
These lower your total income.

Step 6: Find Your AGI

AGI = Total money − adjustments

Step 7: Deductions (Lines 11–12)

  • Standard deduction (easy)
  • Itemized (more detailed)
Pick the bigger one to save money.

Step 8: Calculate Tax (Lines 13–15)

  • Line 13: Your tax
  • Extra taxes if needed
  • Total tax

Step 9: Credits & Payments (Lines 16–20)

  • Tax already paid
  • Credits (money back)
Credits = money back!

Step 10: Refund or Owe (Lines 21–24)

  • Paid too much → refund
  • Paid too little → owe

Step 11: Sign (Line 25)

Don’t forget to sign!

Extra Forms

  • Schedule A – deductions
  • Schedule B – interest
  • Schedule C – business
