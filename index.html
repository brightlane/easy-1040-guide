Step 3: Form 1040 - Lines 1 to 6

Now let's dive into **Form 1040**, which is the core tax form you'll use. These first few lines deal with your income and some basic deductions.

Line 1: Wages, Salaries, Tips

Enter your total wages, salaries, and tips here. This information can be found on **Form W-2**.

Line 2: Taxable Interest

Include all taxable interest income from your savings and investments.

Line 3: Ordinary Dividends

Report any dividend income you’ve received here.

Line 4: IRA Distributions

If you received IRA distributions, report them here.

Line 5: Pensions and Annuities

Report any income from pensions and annuities.

Line 6: Social Security Benefits

Report Social Security benefits here.
